All about Jake (the dog) Dye

About

Jake's primary residence is in Kennesaw, GA. He is presumed to be a mix of the Labrador Retriever and Newfoundland breeds, and he is one hundred percent the bestest doggo. He can usually be found either hanging out inside his house or hunting squirrels in the yard.

History

In 2014, Jake was found as a puppy in a barrel in the woods of a paintball field in Atlanta, GA. It is assumed that he was born in the month of August, therefore his birthday is most frequently celebrated on the first day of August. He lived in Atlanta for three years before moving to Kennesaw.
